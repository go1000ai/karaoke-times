# Karaoke Times — KJ Dashboard Guide

Welcome to your KJ Dashboard! Here's what each section does and how to use it.

---

## 1. Overview

Your home base. See a quick snapshot of your connected venues and recent activity.

---

## 2. My Events

**This is where you create and manage your karaoke nights.**

- Tap **Add Event** to create a new event
- **Pick your venue** from the dropdown (if you're connected to multiple venues)
- **Select the day of week** and optionally set a specific **event date**
- **Pick start and end times** from the dropdowns (30-minute increments, 12 PM – 4 AM)
- Add your name under **KJ / DJ**, plus happy hour details, notes, and event rules (age restriction, dress code, cover charge, etc.)
- Once created, your event card shows up with all the details. You can **edit**, **activate/deactivate**, or **delete** it anytime
- Each event card has a **Create Your Flyer** link that auto-fills the Flyer Generator with your event details
- You can also add **Promos** to each event (e.g., "$3 wells before 9PM") — expand the Promos section on any event card

---

## 3. Media

Upload photos and videos for your venue. These show up on the public venue page so singers can see what the vibe is like.

---

## 4. Flyer Generator

**Create promotional flyers for your events in seconds.**

- Event details auto-fill if you click "Create Your Flyer" from an event card
- Customize colors, add your venue logo, and tweak the text
- Download the finished flyer image to share on social media, group chats, etc.

---

## 5. Song Queue

**Live queue management during your karaoke night.**

- See all song requests from singers at your venue in real time
- Change a singer's status: **Waiting** → **Up Next** → **Now Singing** → **Completed**
- Skip or reorder as needed
- Singers submit requests from their own phone through the app

---

## 6. VirtualDJ

Integration with VirtualDJ software for KJs who use it. Connect your VDJ setup to sync with the Karaoke Times queue.

---

## 7. TV Display

**A full-screen display meant for a TV or projector at your venue.**

- Shows the current singer, who's up next, and the full queue
- Share the TV Display link with your venue — open it on a TV browser or cast it
- Updates in real time as you manage the queue

---

## 8. Bookings

**Manage private event booking requests.**

- Singers and event organizers can request to book you for private karaoke events
- You'll see incoming requests here and can accept or decline
- View your calendar of upcoming booked events

---

## 9. Featured Singers

**Highlight standout performers from your events.**

- Tap **Feature Singer** to spotlight someone
- **Search for the singer by name** (they need a Karaoke Times account)
- Add the **song they performed**, a **YouTube video link** of their performance (optional), and any notes
- Choose the highlight type: **Singer of the Night**, **Weekly Featured**, or **Monthly Featured**
- The singer gets notified and must **approve** before it goes public
- Approved features appear on the public Featured Singers page and the homepage

---

## 10. Connections

**See which venues you're connected to.**

- This shows all the venues where you've been added as a KJ staff member
- The venue owner sends the connection invite; once you accept, the venue appears here
- You can manage events, queue, and media for any venue you're connected to
- Switch between venues using the **venue switcher** at the top of the sidebar

---

## 11. My Profile

**Your personal account settings.**

- Update your display name, avatar, phone, address, website, and social links
- Manage your public KJ page (bio, genres, equipment, etc.) which appears at your unique KJ profile URL

---

## Quick Tips

- **Switching venues:** If you work at multiple spots, use the venue dropdown at the top-left of the sidebar to switch your active venue
- **Creating events fast:** Fill in the form once → your event card has a "Create Your Flyer" button that auto-fills everything into the Flyer Generator
- **Queue during a show:** Keep the Song Queue page open on your laptop/tablet. Singers request songs from their phones and they appear in your queue instantly
- **TV at the venue:** Open the TV Display on the venue's TV browser — it auto-updates as you manage the queue
